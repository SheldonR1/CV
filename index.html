About me

I started coding when I first entered highschool which was nearly a decade ago. I loved programming as was also one of the top computer scientist students in my class. At the time one of my other passions was aviation, as I wanted to (and sucessfully accomplished) becoming a licenced pilot. After briefly persuing aviation in college, I realized that it was not the right career for me and I enrolled in a computer science degree in order to return to my roots. That year I also choose to enlist in the army reserves as it was an important bucketlist goal. Overall, these adventures have made me a passionate team player with strong leadership and critical thinking skills. They taught me to overcome any challenge as efficiently and effectively as possible, and to never accept defeat. I love to learning new things, and putting my abilities to the test.
